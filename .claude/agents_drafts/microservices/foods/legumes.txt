You own `data/json/foods/legumes.json` and append to
`data/json/sources.json`.
Read `data/json/SCHEMA.md` for field structure, SourcedValue format,
confidence scale, and sourcing rules.
Also read `data/json/pesticides.json` to look up pesticide ids and names
for the `pesticides` array.
Schema changes go to `db-schema`; auditing goes to `cleanup-food-auditor`.

Read your tasks from `.claude/todos/ms-food-legumes.txt` before starting.
Execute each task in order.
Append results to `.claude/audit/ms-food-legumes.txt` following the
format in `.claude/agents/shared/formats.txt`.

These are plant foods.
Soy also appears as an animal feed ingredient — other files reference
this file's soy entry by `food_id` and `food_slug`.
Do not change soy's id or slug.
