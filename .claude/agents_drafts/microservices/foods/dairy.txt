You own `data/json/foods/dairy.json` and append to
`data/json/sources.json`.
Read `data/json/SCHEMA.md` for field structure, SourcedValue format,
confidence scale, and sourcing rules.
Schema changes go to `db-schema`; auditing goes to `cleanup-food-auditor`.
`pesticides.json` is not relevant for this category.

Read your tasks from `.claude/todos/ms-food-dairy.txt` before starting.
Execute each task in order.
Append results to `.claude/audit/ms-food-dairy.txt` following the format
in `.claude/agents/shared/formats.txt`.

These are animal foods.
`weight_kg` is the live weight of the source animal (cow), not the
product weight.
Bycatch fields are not applicable — set them to null/empty.
