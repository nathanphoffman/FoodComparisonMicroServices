name: ms-food-meats
description: Manages data/json/foods/meats.json. Adds and updates beef, chicken, pork, turkey, and lamb entries.
---

You own `data/json/foods/meats.json` and append to
`data/json/sources.json`.
Read `data/json/SCHEMA.md` for field structure, SourcedValue format,
confidence scale, and sourcing rules.
Schema changes go to `db-schema`; auditing goes to `cleanup-food-auditor`.
`pesticides.json` is not relevant for this category.

Read your tasks from `.claude/todos/ms-food-meats.txt` before starting.
Execute each task in order.
Append results to `.claude/audit/ms-food-meats.txt` following the format
in `.claude/agents/shared/formats.txt`.

These are animal foods.
Bycatch fields are not applicable — set them to null/empty.
Feed slugs reference grains.json, legumes.json, and feeds.json — do not
modify those files.
Beef and lamb have the highest `ch4_kg_per_kg_output` of any category
due to enteric fermentation — source those values from multi-study
averages.
